+1Eyewear — Account Deletion Request

Developer: PlusOne Import
App: +1Eyewear (com.plus1eyewear.app)

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to plusoneeyewear@gmail.com with subject: “Account Deletion – +1Eyewear”.
  2. Include the following:
    • Full name
    • Email used in the app (or Google account email)
    • Country (optional)
  3. We will verify your identity and process the request.

Processing time: within 30 days of verifying your identity.

What we delete

  • Account profile and authentication linkages
  • Personal data stored with your account (e.g., saved preferences, support history)
  • Any other personal data tied to your account in our systems

Don’t have an account?

If you never created an account in +1Eyewear, you may still request deletion of any personal data you shared with us (e.g., support emails). Follow the same steps above.
